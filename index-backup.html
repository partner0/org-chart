Proposed PDE Org Improvements

Rationale

  • Further optimizing PDE effectiveness: We get better at taking larger product bets more often (like Neyoba) by reducing overhead for ICs and improving alignment driven by Director/Staff leads. This increased focus and ruthless prioritization will result in higher impact on listings, GBV, and take rate.
  • Catalyzing and capitalizing on AI transformation: We create a growth opportunity for Paula, with chances to lead day-to-day management and engineering operations, and rebalancing Francois' impact toward strategy and technical leadership while allowing him to delegate more. This will result in stronger leadership continuity, succession planning, and more space to push further toward AI transformation, and the largely untapped productivity potential that exists there.
  • Balancing the load between EMs: We target an EM:IC ratio of 1:10, leveraging our current EM staff without needing additional managers. This rebalancing will enable more sustainable spans of control and improved retention. This will result in reduced overhead and an environment that further enables ICs to increase their contributions and focus their energy on the most impactful bets.
